## A History of Technology and People

**The history of animation is a history of technology:** the manufacture of lenses; invention of photography; of celluloid as a clear, plastic medium; sound recording and synchronization; color photography; television and the cathode ray tube (CRT); computers and the advent of generative AI —all have pushed animation in new directions and the process has not yet ended.

**The history of animation is a history of people:** lone artists; small studios; businesses and sprawling commercial empires! Fortunes have been made _and lost— in animation. One of the driving forces in the history if animation is costs. There is an economic history here as well.
